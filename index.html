Credit Card Analysis

Initial credit purchase stands at $6000 dollars for 3-month initial onboarding of new customers. On further uncovering of the data, we realized that we have a churn rate of 16%, depending on various factors such as dependents. The average age of our customers is 46, with a credit limit of $8500. As we begin to uncover more patterns in the data set, we realize that there are no active customers above 11k dollars. The new customers tend to churn in the new year, showing a 55% drop most likely due to holiday expenses. Target them with cash backs and loyalty points, followed by user surveys.
